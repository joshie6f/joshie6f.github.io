'Mr Robot' Season 4, The end of the Road!

AUG 30, 2018 by joshua Solanke



Mr.Robot,

Mr.Robot is a show in which You (The Viewer) accompany an anti-social cybersecurity engineer 'Elliot Alderson' (Rami Malek) on his day to day journey as his Imaginary Friend. our main protagonist works for the business 'E-Corp' when the sun shines, but at night he and a hidden hacker group - lead by a questionable leader - strive to take down 'E-corp' under the wraps.

The exciting news of Season 4's release brought about some other news. Christian Slater has told fans that he believes that season 4 will bring about the end of mr.robot "I believe that will be the last season.

Sam [Esmail] always said it was going to be somewhere in that zone, and he didn't want to go further than what he could creatively contribute to that storyline. So, I think that Season 4 will be it. I think they're in the writers' room, as we speak, putting it all together, but i have no idea what it's gonna be, or if i'm gonna be floating in and out of scenes." Christian Slater said This during an interview

The Release Date

At the time, Mr.Robot's 4th Season dose not have an offical release date, but The USA Network however, said it should air sometime in 2019.
